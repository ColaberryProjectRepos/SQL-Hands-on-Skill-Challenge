Explanation:

The question asks for the list of accounts that had a positive net charge on their valid 201712 invoice. This is solved by joining accounts table and net_invoice_info table. If positive net charge which is net_invoice_cents is greater than zero or not equals to zero, net_invoice_cents is then filtered by where clause (net_invoice_cents >0) and (validation_invoice_cent >0) assuming validation_invoice_cent = 0, meaning invalid invoices. Original_year_month is filtered for 201712 by where clause.

Sum of net_invoice_cents were listed in the select statement as Total_net_invoice_cents, Sum of messages_cents_invoiced were listed in the select statement as Total_messages_cents, Sum of validation_cents_invoiced were listed in the select statement as Total_Validation_cens.

For the 2nd part of the question, whether they paid more on messages or validations, case statement is used to answer that question. When sum of messages_cents_invoiced is greater than or equals to net_invoice_cents gives the message ‘they paid right on messages’ and when sum of the validation_cents_invoiced is less than or equal to sum of net_invoice_cents then this condition gives the message ‘they paid right on validation’ , else given the condition is invalid. The query is then grouped by account_name, account_id, original_year_month and ordered by account_name and account_id

Temporary table is used to solve this problem. Sum of net_invoice_cents times .01 was taken as the revenue and regarded as the Spending in Dollars.
